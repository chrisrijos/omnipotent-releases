How Aiegis enforces compliance

Compliance isn’t a checkbox — it’s an architecture decision. These six principles are baked into every layer of Aiegis.

Local-First Execution

Aiegis runs as a single binary on your infrastructure. No cloud callbacks, no SaaS dependencies. Your data stays on your hardware.

PII Redaction at the Edge

All 18 HIPAA identifiers, credit card numbers, SSNs, and custom patterns are stripped before any token leaves your network perimeter.

Zero-Retention Mode

Optional zero-retention logging ensures no prompt content is stored after the request completes. Hash-tokenized summaries preserve auditability.

Immutable Audit Trail

Every scan decision is logged: timestamp, rule ID, verdict, latency, hash of redacted content. Tamper-evident. Export-ready for auditors.

Data Residency Controls

Route AI traffic to specific model providers based on data classification. Block cross-border egress. Enforce per-tenant isolation policies.

Auditor-Ready Reports

Generate compliance reports mapping Aiegis controls to framework requirements. GDPR Article 30 records, HIPAA audit logs, SOC2 evidence packages.

Framework coverage

Every framework mapped to specific Aiegis controls. No vague claims — concrete article-by-article coverage.
