Pattern Engine

Write rules for your domain.

Define custom detection patterns in TOML. Combine semantic similarity, regex matching, and intent classification in a single rule. Test against sample inputs, then deploy to every node with one click.

  • Semantic similarity matching with configurable thresholds
  • Regex and intent classification conditions
  • Per-pattern alert routing to Slack, Discord, PagerDuty
  • Version-controlled with rollback support
